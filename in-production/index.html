In Production

In Production:

Headless (feature, Spring 2015)
Feature length film starring Shane Beasley, based on the fake slasher film “Headless” as featured in Scott Schirmer’s found.  Headless is written by Nathan Erdel, directed by Arthur Cullipher, produced by Scott Schirmer and Kara Erdel, and features cinematography by Leya Taylor. Headless was funded, in part, by a successful Kickstarter campaign, and will be released on DVD, BluRay, and limited edition VHS by Vultra Video. Headless is a join production between Forbidden Films and Gentleman Monster Productions.

Recently Completed:

Unwelcome. (short, April 2014)
Short film starring Benjamin Schuetz, Lindsey McDermott, Kate Braun (found.), and Victor Miller (screenwriter, Friday the 13th). Unwelcome is written and directed by Nathan Erdel, produced by Kara Erdel, and features cinematography by Leya Taylor (found.). Unwelcome has played the Indie Film Con and the Innovative Film Festival, and is currently being considered at other film festivals.

The Making of “found.” (documentary, Spring 2014)
Behind-the scenes documentary detailing the making of Scott Schirmer’s found., an independent horror film shot in Indiana and the winner of Elvira’s Horror Hunt. Found is currently playing in film festivals. This documentary is featured on the UK & Australian DVD of Found, released by Monster Pictures and XLrator Media.

“PSA” – Commercial for “Tattoo Charlie’s” (commercial, Summer 2014)
Commercial/PSA for Tattoo Charlie’s, in Kentucky. Directed and edited by Nathan Erdel, produced by Kara Erdel, written by Nathan Erdel and Kara Erdel, with cinematography by Leya Taylor, audio by Kevin Copeland, and special make-up effects by The Clockwerk Creature Company (Arthur Cullipher and Shane Beasley). The commercial starred Abigail Copeland, Kevin Copeland, Nathan Erdel, and Kate Braun.
